Introduction

A look at Wonders of the World

﻿

The wonders of the world are fascinating phenomenon. Some of the wonders were created in ancient times, some were created in modern times, some are natural, and some are technological. These phenomena are marvels that include statues, waterfalls, bridges, dams, lighthouses, just to name a few. What makes the wonders interesting is, when you look at them, you begin to think about the kind of work that must have went into making them or, in nature’s case, marvel at their lively beauty. This website displays just a few of these magnificent wonders. Take time to look around the site and, most importantly, enjoy!
